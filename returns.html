RETURNS POLICY

Return Window

We want you to love your EiOC pieces. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we accept returns within 30 days of the original purchase date for unworn items in their original condition with all tags attached.

What Can Be Returned

  • Regular-priced items in original condition
  • Items with all original tags attached
  • Unworn, unwashed, and undamaged items
  • Items in original packaging

What Cannot Be Returned

  • Sale items (marked with "Final Sale")
  • Personalized or custom items
  • Accessories (jewelry, bags, scarves)
  • Items that have been worn, washed, or damaged
  • Items without original tags or packaging
  • Items purchased more than 30 days ago

How to Return

Step 1: Contact Us

Email us at returns@eioc.com or call us at 1-800-EIOC-123 to initiate your return. Please include your order number and the reason for return.

Step 2: Package Your Item

Securely package your item in its original packaging with all tags attached. Include a copy of your order confirmation or packing slip.

Step 3: Ship Your Return

Ship your return to our returns address using a trackable shipping method. We recommend using the same carrier as your original order.

Step 4: Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days. You'll receive an email confirmation.

Return Shipping

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective or we sent the wrong item. We recommend using a trackable shipping method to ensure your return reaches us safely.

Refund Information

  • Refunds will be processed to the original payment method
  • Processing time: 5-7 business days after we receive your return
  • Original shipping costs are non-refundable
  • Return shipping costs are not refunded unless the item is defective

Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. To exchange an item for a different size or color, please return the original item and place a new order for the desired item.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately at support@eioc.com with photos of the damage. We'll provide a prepaid return label and process your refund or replacement as quickly as possible.

International Returns

International customers are responsible for return shipping costs and any applicable customs duties or taxes. Returns must be shipped within 30 days of delivery.
